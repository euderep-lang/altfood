# Altfood Design System

## Brand Identity

- **Product name:** altfood (lowercase logotype)
- **Tagline:** Substituição Alimentar Inteligente
- **Positioning:** Brazilian health-tech platform connecting nutritionists with evidence-based food substitution tools
- **Tone:** Clean, scientific, trustworthy. Organic warmth with precision — approachable professional, not clinical/sterile.
